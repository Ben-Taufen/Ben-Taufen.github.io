About

Who am I?

Growing up in Monticello, Minnesota, I enjoyed my time by hanging out with friends, playing sports and video games, and playing instruments such as drums and guitar. I am currently a senior at the University of St. Thomas. Here I am studying computer science along with a mathematics minor. After graduation, I plan to apply the knowledge I have learned inside and outside the classroom to my career as a Software Engineer.
